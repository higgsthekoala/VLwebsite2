Where Sound Comes Alive

Step inside our world-class facilities where cutting-edge technology meets artistic vision. Our studio is designed to capture every nuance of sonic expression, from intimate acoustic performances to epic orchestral recordings.

Our Philosophy

At Sonar Music, we believe that exceptional sound design begins with exceptional spaces. Our studio is more than just a collection of equipment—it's a carefully crafted environment where creativity flourishes and sonic possibilities are limitless.

Every element of our facility has been designed with one goal in mind: to provide the perfect acoustic environment for capturing and creating the most nuanced, powerful, and emotionally resonant soundscapes possible.

Studio Capabilities

Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with the latest technology and designed for maximum creative flexibility.

Dolby Atmos Mixing

Immersive audio mixing with our Dolby Atmos certified suite, featuring 64-channel mixing capabilities and pristine acoustic treatment for the most accurate monitoring environment.

Live Room Recording

Multiple isolation booths and a spacious live room designed for recording everything from solo instruments to full ensembles, with variable acoustics and premium microphone selection.

Sound Design Suite

Dedicated sound design workstations equipped with the latest software, synthesizers, and processing hardware for creating unique soundscapes and custom audio elements.

Post-Production

Complete post-production facilities with video sync capabilities, ADR booths, and specialized equipment for film, television, and commercial audio finishing.

Mastering Suite

Professional mastering environment with reference-grade monitoring, analog and digital processing chains, and acoustically optimized listening position for final polish.

Archive & Storage

Comprehensive digital asset management with redundant backup systems, ensuring your projects are safely stored and easily accessible for future reference or revision.

Our Process

From concept to completion, our proven methodology ensures exceptional results on every project.

01

Discovery & Planning

We begin every project with in-depth consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and creative goals. This phase includes script analysis, reference gathering, and technical planning.

02

Pre-Production

Detailed preparation including equipment selection, studio booking, session scheduling, and creative development. We ensure every element is perfectly aligned before recording begins.

03

Recording & Creation

The magic happens in our state-of-the-art studios. Whether recording live instruments, creating sound design, or producing electronic elements, we capture every nuance with precision.

04

Post-Production

Mixing, mastering, and final polish bring everything together. Our post-production suite ensures perfect sync, optimal levels, and industry-standard delivery formats.

05

Delivery & Support

Final delivery in all required formats, complete with documentation and ongoing support. We remain available for revisions and future project collaboration.
